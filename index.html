How to play

Objective

Your goal is to create a single continuous path from the Start Room to the Goal Room by arranging rooms so that their doors (yellow lines) connect properly.

Tile Types

  • Green Room (S): Start room. Fixed in place and cannot be moved.
  • Red Room (G): Goal room. Fixed in place and cannot be moved.
  • Purple Room (H): Hidden room. A fixed hint tile. There's at least 2 rooms near hidden rooms.
  • Gray Rooms: Fixed normal rooms.
  • Blue Rooms: Movable puzzle pieces you must place on the board.
  • Empty Slots: Black tiles where you can place rooms.

Placing Puzzle Pieces

  • Drag a room from the piece list below and drop it onto an empty slot on the board.
  • Rooms already placed on the board (blue rooms) can be dragged and moved freely.
  • Tiles with an orange border are fixed and cannot be moved.
  • Adjacent rooms must have matching door directions to form valid connections.
  • To complete the puzzle, all movable pieces must be used.

Checking the Solution

  • Click Check Solution to verify your board.
  • The puzzle is solved only if:
  • 1) No pieces remain unused
  • 2) All adjacent rooms have consistent door connections
  • 3) There is a valid path from Start to Goal
